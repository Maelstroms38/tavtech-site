ABOUT TAVhacks

We believe in impact. We have convened young technologists and entrepreneurs from all over the world in Tel Aviv from NYU, Brown, Cornell, Harvard, MIT, and 26 other college campuses and 7 countries to ignite grassroots social change.


Diverse, technical entrepreneurs. Competitors in the hackathon come from a variety of academic backgrounds in cybersecurity, data science, big data, artificial intelligence, and programming - as well as non-technical fields.

Who We Are

TAVtech Ventures Global (TAVtech) is a nonprofit that operates a coding and entrepreneurship fellowship which brings students from top US universities to Israel over winter break to learn high demand coding skills while being immersed in Israel’s startup economy.

Intensive Courses
Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, Coding Crash Course, Cybersecurity, and Data Science
