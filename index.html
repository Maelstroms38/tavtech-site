# Code for the Planet

ABOUT TAVhack

We have brought 80 of the brightest young minds to Israel on the TAVtech Fellowship where after the course of 4 weeks of intensive computer science instruction and an immersive exposure to Israel’s startup ecosystem, they will apply what they learned to make the world a better place at TAVhack, TAVtech’s social impact hackathon.

Who We Are

TAVtech Ventures Global ("TAVtech") is a nonprofit that operates a coding and entrepreneurship fellowship which brings students from top US universities to Israel over winter break to learn high demand coding skills while being immersed in Israel’s startup economy.

Intensive Courses
Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, Coding Crash Course, Cybersecurity, and Data Science.
